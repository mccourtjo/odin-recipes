Chocolate brownie

A chocolate treat, great with fresh cream or ice cream -serve warm.

  • Ingredients

  • 200g flour
  • 400g of butter
  • Chocolate chips
  • Chocolate powder

  1. Steps

  2. mix chocolate powder, flour , butter in mixing bowl until smooth
  3. Add choc chips to mixture
  4. Spread evenly on buttered baking tray
  5. Place in over at 200 degrees for 15 mins
  6. Serve with cream or ice cream
