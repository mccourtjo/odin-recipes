Lasagna

A delicious italian treat for warm winter nights, great with a bottle of Rioja.

  • Ingredients

  • 500g Beef Mince
  • Tin of chopped tomatoes
  • Onion
  • Italian Herbs

  1. Steps

  2. Add a splash of oil to a frying pan and fry mince until light brown
  3. Add tomatoes, herbs and beef to a large saucepan
  4. Chop and fry onion
  5. Add onion to saucepan
  6. Simmer until mince tender (around 45 minutes)
